# Chair records

This folder holds public records about the IATF chair role:

- Term announcements and end-of-term notes.
- Election calls and results.
- Step-down or extraordinary-election notices, when they happen.

The chair role itself, the election rules, and the continuity policy are
described at <https://iatf.cc/governance/> under "Chair".

## File naming

`YYYY-MM-DD-short-slug.md`, ordered by the date the record applies to.

## Minimal entry shape

```markdown
---
date: 2026-12-15
type: election-result | term | step-down | extraordinary
chair: <github-handle>
---

Short description of what was decided and why. Keep it factual.
```

The chair is the public point of contact for IATF. See
<mailto:contact@iatf.cc>.
